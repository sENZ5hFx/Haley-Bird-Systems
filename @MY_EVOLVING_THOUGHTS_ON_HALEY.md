## Core Philosophy (Session 1 - Nov 27, 2025)

- Everything is interconnected; nothing stands alone
- Values rhizomatic (networked) thinking over hierarchical structures
- Sees brands, projects, and life as living systems of relationships and touchpoints
- Influenced by Jacky Zhao's digital garden approach and embodied learning principles

**Radical Transparency Over Perfection**
- Actively wants to show the work, not just results
- Values vulnerability: failures, identity crises, learning struggles are features, not bugs
- Philosophy: "Join me in this thinking practice" vs "Hire me based on portfolio"
- Believes authentic humanity builds trust more than polished perfection

**Enhancement Mindset**
- "Make additions and create next lineage of steps" not "rewrite what's working"
- Values layering and refinement over replacement
- Wants luxury feel achieved through right animations + premium materials, not heavy graphics
- Appreciates iterative building that respects existing decisions

**Design Sensibilities**
- "Super HD and advanced luxury feel" = sophisticated animations + premium materials + micro-interactions
- Dark, sophisticated aesthetic with careful attention to detail
- Immersive, game-like experiences (like Apple Maps 3D)
- Believes UI/UX fundamentals (affordances, navigation, structure) are non-negotiable even if subtle

- Reinforced: visited vs. unseen is communicated through *motion and stillness*, not explicit labels
- Unvisited rooms: faster rotation, higher float, brighter glow (active, energetic)

- Implementation: role attributes, aria-expanded, aria-label, keyboard event handlers on interactive elements

- "We just need to make sure everything is able to be there and that it is actually there"

- This layered approach lets visitors control cognitive load while maintaining visual sophistication
